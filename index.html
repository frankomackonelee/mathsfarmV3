Welcome to the Maths Farm

Down on the farm you can turn a handful of your own questions into some great resources, saving yourself hours. You can save yourself even more time by using the questions already provided by the farm!

There are four resourse types you can convert sets of questions into which are:

  • Questions and Answers Simply display your questions on screen with the answers ready to be displayed at a click
  • Catch Phrase A class plenary where pupils need to answer questions to reveal a visual puzzle of your choice
  • Find the phrase Hide a hidden message which will amuse your class. They need to answer the questions to find out what it is
  • Class Bingo Print out numbered bingo cards to help you assess how pupils are getting on in this classic game
